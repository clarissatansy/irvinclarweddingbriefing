Stop Highlights

Day 1 — Pennsylvania

York & Lancaster

Lavender farms, dairy farm ice cream, an Utz factory tour, Amish market, and Wyndridge Farm's farm-to-table dinner and craft ciders in a 100-year-old barn.

Day 2 — West Virginia & Virginia

Shepherdstown & Harpers Ferry

WV's oldest cobblestone town, the dramatic point where two rivers meet, Jefferson Rock, and Luray Caverns' extraordinary underground formations.

Day 2 — Virginia

Luray Caverns

Vast underground cave systems with soaring stalactite formations, the famous Great Stalacpipe Organ, and the charming farm-to-table food scene of downtown Luray.

Day 3 — Virginia

Shenandoah National Park

Skyline Drive overlooks, three beginner hiking trails, kayaking on the Shenandoah River, horseback riding, and stargazing at Big Meadows under the Milky Way.

Day 4 — Washington DC

Washington DC

Your DC, your way — since you've been before, keep it relaxed. National Mall at dusk, Call Your Mother bagels, Ben's Chili Bowl, and oysters at Old Ebbitt Grill. Optional Leesburg outlet shopping en route.

Day 5 — Maryland

Annapolis

A charming colonial harbour town on the Chesapeake Bay — the perfect final stop before heading home. Finish with steamed Maryland blue crabs with Old Bay, the best farewell feast imaginable.

York, Lancaster & Gettysburg

▶ Tuesday 11 Aug  |  Philadelphia → York ~1.5 hrs  |  York → Lancaster ~45 mins  |  Lancaster → Gettysburg ~1 hr (drive-through)  |  → Overnight ~30 mins

Lancaster Central Market

☸

Stops & Activities

Morning

Castle Creek Lavender Farm ✦

U-pick lavender fields in full August bloom, adorable alpacas and farm animals, plus a shop with lavender soaps, wreaths, and sometimes lavender ice cream.

Mid-morning

Perrydell Farm Dairy ✦

A beloved local farm institution — fresh cheese, flavoured milk, and farm-made ice cream straight from the source. A must for foodies.

Late morning

Utz Factory Tour

Free self-guided tour of the working factory where Utz pretzels, Hanover Potato Chips, and Zapp's chips are all made. Open Tue–Thu — perfectly timed for your trip!

Midday

Lancaster Central Market

A quick graze through one of America's oldest farmers markets — fresh bread, local cheeses, whoopie pies, and shoofly pie from the Amish stalls.

Afternoon

Tanger Outlets Lancaster ✦

65+ brand name stores including Coach, Calvin Klein, Nike, Pottery Barn, Under Armour, Banana Republic, Tommy Hilfiger, and West Elm — all up to 70% off. A great afternoon stop before heading to Wyndridge for dinner. tanger.com/lancaster →

Afternoon

Optional: Gettysburg Detour (~1 hr out of the way)

Optional Detour

If you fancy a scenic drive through American history, the Gettysburg battlefield is a beautiful landscape even from the car — no need to stop long, just roll through with the windows down. The Outlet Shoppes at Gettysburg is also right there with 40+ stores including Columbia, Old Navy, and Under Armour if you want a quick browse. Otherwise, skip it and head straight to Wyndridge for an earlier, more relaxed evening. Outlet Shoppes →

Evening

Wyndridge Farm ✦

Dinner at the farm-to-table restaurant in a 100-year-old restored barn. House-smoked meats, seasonal ingredients, brick-fired pizzas, and farm-crafted ciders brewed on site. The biergarten with bistro lights and rolling Pennsylvania hills is stunning.

♡

Food & Drink

Morning treat

Perrydell Farm Dairy

Farm-fresh ice cream, cheese, and flavoured milk straight from the farm. Start the trip right.

Midday graze

Lancaster Central Market

Pick up fresh bread, cheese, pastries, and Amish baked goods to snack on through the afternoon.

Dinner ⭐

Wyndridge Farm ✦

Scratch kitchen with seasonal farm-fresh ingredients, house-smoked meats, brick-fired pizza, and farm-crafted ciders and beers brewed on the property. The Honey Lavender Cider is a must. Open Wed–Sun from 11:30am.

Must Try

Shoofly pie, whoopie pies, Amish chicken pot pie, fresh apple cider, and Wyndridge's rotating seasonal ciders — especially the Honey Lavender Cider in summer.

Stops & Activities

Morning

Shepherdstown, WV (~2 hrs)

WV's oldest town. Coffee at Betty's, stroll cobblestone streets, browse galleries and bookshops. Quick drink by the Bavarian Inn's Potomac infinity pool.

Mid-morning

Harpers Ferry — The Point ✦

Where the Shenandoah and Potomac Rivers merge. Jefferson Rock hike, Lower Town historic district, Virginius Island Trail.

Afternoon

Luray Caverns ✦

Vast underground cave systems with extraordinary stalactite formations and the Great Stalacpipe Organ. Allow 1–1.5 hrs.

Evening

Downtown Luray

Stroll Main Street, browse boutiques, unwind with a craft beer at Hawksbill Brewing Co. before dinner.

♡

Food & Drink

Breakfast — Shepherdstown

Betty's ✦

Home of the world's best sausage gravy over hot biscuits. Jumbo lump crab cakes also stellar. Cash only.

Snack — Harpers Ferry

Bolivar Bakery

Cult cinnamon buns, sourdough rosemary olive bread, and the unmissable Pretzel Samosa.

Lunch — Harpers Ferry

Kelley Farm Kitchen

Farm-to-table with inventive plant-based dishes. Non-vegans leave just as impressed.

Drinks — Luray

Hawksbill Brewing Co.

Outdoor patio with mountain views. Perfect post-caverns unwind.

Dinner — Luray

The Chop House Bistro ✦

Top foodie pick in Luray — farm-to-table, elevated seasonal ingredients, great cocktails.

Dessert

Flotzie's Ice Cream

A beloved Luray local favourite. Simple and iconic.

A historic Georgian Revival inn on rolling hills near the Shenandoah River. Outdoor pools, fire pits, walking paths, hammock garden, two restaurants including the Speakeasy bar. 2 mins from Luray Caverns.

💡 Tip

Dark Hollow Falls is easy going down but steep on the return climb — wear good shoes and take your time. All three trails together = ~5 miles and 3.5–4 hrs, leaving the afternoon free for kayaking and the evening for stargazing.

☻

Other Activities

Morning

Skyline Drive

105 miles along the Blue Ridge crest with 75 overlooks. Enter at Thornton Gap and pull over whenever you want.

Afternoon

Kayaking / Tubing ✦

Float the Shenandoah River with Shenandoah River Outfitters. A warm August afternoon on the river is unforgettable.

Afternoon

Horseback Riding

Fort Valley Ranch guided trail rides into the George Washington National Forest. Beginner-friendly and very romantic.

Evening

Wildlife Spotting

Deer, wild turkeys, and black bears commonly spotted along Skyline Drive in August.

Night

Stargazing at Big Meadows ✦

The Milky Way is visible with the naked eye on a clear August night. Bring a blanket. One of the most romantic moments of the trip.

♡

Food & Drink

Lunch — Inside the Park

Pollock Dining Room, Skyland Lodge ✦

Regional farm-to-fork with stunning valley views. The Blackberry Ice Cream Pie is non-negotiable. Pork shank is consistently outstanding.

Drinks — Luray Area

River Hill Wine & Spirits

Family-owned farm winery along the Shenandoah River. Small-batch wines and corn whiskey.

Must Try in the Valley

Homemade apple pie, apple butter, farm-raised trout, and fresh apple cider. Pick up apple butter from a roadside stall as a souvenir.

Overnight — Inside the Park

Stay at Big Meadows Lodge or Skyland Lodge on Skyline Drive. Essential for the stargazing — avoids a late-night mountain drive and puts you right at the heart of the park.

The National Mall at dusk

☸

Stops & Activities

Morning

Leisurely Lodge Breakfast

No rush — enjoy one last mountain morning before the drive to DC.

En Route

Optional: Montpelier Area Detour (+1.5–2 hrs)

Optional Detour

Skip the house tour — instead walk the 3.5-mile Montpelier Loop Trail through wildflower meadows, then head 15 mins to Barboursville Vineyards for a wine tasting on a stunning Virginia estate. Adds ~1.5–2 hrs to the day but is a beautiful Virginia countryside experience. barboursville.com →

En Route

Optional: Leesburg Premium Outlets (35 miles from DC)

Optional Shopping Stop

100+ designer and name-brand stores including Armani, Burberry, Coach, Kate Spade, Michael Kors, Polo Ralph Lauren, Tory Burch, UGG, and Williams-Sonoma — up to 65% off retail. Only 35 miles from DC, so you can shop for 2 hours and still arrive in time for dinner. Open Mon–Sat 10am–9pm. premiumoutlets.com →

Afternoon

Your DC, Your Way

Since you've both been before, revisit a favourite neighbourhood, museum, or spot.

Evening

National Mall at Dusk ✦

A slow evening stroll as the monuments light up — one of the most beautiful urban walks in America at golden hour.

♡

Food & Drink

Brunch

Call Your Mother Deli ✦

Wildly popular Jewish-style deli. The pastrami, egg and cheese on sesame bagel is one of the best breakfast sandwiches in the city.

Lunch

Ben's Chili Bowl

A DC institution on U Street since 1958. The half-smoke draped in chili is the quintessential DC food experience.

Dinner

Old Ebbitt Grill ✦

Washington's oldest saloon, steps from the White House. Seasonal oysters, jumbo lump crab cakes, classic American charm. Make a reservation.

Must Try in DC

The half-smoke at Ben's Chili Bowl, Chesapeake Bay oysters, and Maryland blue crab cakes at Old Ebbitt Grill.

Stops & Activities

Morning

Slow Morning in DC

Coffee, a last stroll, no rush. Soak up the final morning of the honeymoon before hitting the road.

Midday

Annapolis, Maryland ✦

A charming colonial harbour town on the Chesapeake Bay. Wander the waterfront and find a great spot for the farewell lunch.

Afternoon

Drive Home to Philadelphia

A comfortable 2-hour drive home — windows down, good music, and a week of beautiful memories.

♡

Food & Drink

Breakfast — DC

Call Your Mother Deli

If you didn't make it yesterday — go early to beat the queue. Worth every minute of the wait.

Lunch — Annapolis

Maryland Blue Crab ✦

Steamed blue crabs with Old Bay seasoning at a waterfront restaurant. Messy, communal, absolutely delicious. The perfect final meal.

The Farewell Feast

Maryland blue crabs are the star of Annapolis. Order them whole, get your hands dirty, and savour every bite.
